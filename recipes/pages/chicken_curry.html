Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

Chicken Curry is a flavorful dish made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a spiced onion-tomato base. Popular across South Asia, this comforting dish pairs perfectly with rice or flatbreads like naan and roti.

The rich gravy, infused with aromatic spices, makes this a hearty and satisfying meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken (bone-in or boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds.
  2. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown. Stir in garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute.
  3. Add turmeric, coriander, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Stir in the tomato puree and cook until the oil begins to separate from the masala.
  5. Add chicken pieces and coat them in the spice mixture. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Pour in water, cover, and let the chicken simmer until fully cooked and tender (about 20 minutes).
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or naan.
